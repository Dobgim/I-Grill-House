Our Story

More Than Just a Meal

Born in the hills of Bamenda, I Grill House has been the go-to spot for families, friends, and food lovers since 2015. We believe great food starts with great fire — and we never compromise on either.

10+ Years of Flame

Authentic Grill Culture in the Heart of Bamenda

Nestled along Commercial Avenue, Bamenda, I Grill House is where the aroma of slow-charcoal grilling draws you in and the flavours keep you coming back. Our chefs use traditional recipes handed down through generations, combined with the finest locally sourced meats and produce.

From our signature Suya Platter to whole grilled tilapia, every dish is crafted with passion, precision, and plenty of fire.
